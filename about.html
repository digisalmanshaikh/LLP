Leadership & Expertise

Ms. Gayathri Srinivasan

CA – Chartered AccountantStrategic CFO AdvisorAuthor & Thought Leader25+ Years Global Experience

Ms. Gayathri Srinivasan is a seasoned Chartered Accountant and Strategic CFO Advisor with over 25 years of experience spanning multiple industries and geographies. She has held senior financial leadership roles, advised boards and promoters, and guided businesses through complex financial transformations.

Her expertise spans CFO services, capital markets advisory, IPO structuring, risk management, and governance consulting — making her a trusted partner for entrepreneurs, CEOs, and boards.

She is also the author of “Strategies for Success – Lessons for Entrepreneurs” — a widely respected guide that distills years of real-world experience into actionable wisdom for business leaders.
